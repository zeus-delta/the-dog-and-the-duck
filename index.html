Welcome to The Dog & Duck

A warm, traditional English pub in the heart of Shirley. Known for hearty homemade food, friendly atmosphere, and a blend of English and Polish flavours.

Whether you're here for a Sunday roast, a pint with friends, or a taste of authentic Polish cuisine, we make everyone feel at home.
